✨ Game Features

⚡ Fast-Paced Gameplay

Endless runner mechanics with dynamic obstacles, enemies, and challenging missions that keep you engaged.

🏔️ Nepal-Inspired World

Explore stunning jungle trails, ancient temple ruins, Himalayan regions, and hidden villages in a low-poly stylized world.

📸 Wildlife Photography

Unlock rewards by photographing rare wildlife. Complete photography missions and build your wildlife gallery.

🌐 Offline + Online

Play completely offline or sync online for cross-platform progress, leaderboards, and community challenges.

📋 Mission System

Dynamic daily and weekly missions with escalating difficulty. Complete objectives to earn rewards and unlock new areas.

📱 Cross-Platform

Play on Android and Web (PWA). Seamless experience across devices with Firebase cloud sync.
