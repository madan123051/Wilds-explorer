🎮 Game Features

🏃

Fast-Paced Gameplay

Endless runner mechanics with mission-based objectives. Collect coins, avoid obstacles, face enemies.

🌿

Nepal-Inspired World

Explore jungles, rivers, mountain trails, villages, and ancient temple ruins inspired by Nepal.

📸

Wildlife Photography

Play as a wildlife photographer/explorer with camera backpack. Discover unique wildlife.

💾

Offline + Online

Play offline anytime. Sync progress to the Wildsaura ecosystem when connected.

🏯

Mission System

Complete daily missions, unlock rewards, progress through story-driven levels.

📱

Cross-Platform

Android, Web (PWA), and future iOS. Low-poly optimized for mobile.
